How this works: EMIs are assumed paid on schedule and never bounced, so you only log rate changes, EMI revisions and prepayments — the tool replays your loan from the start month to today to show interest paid and balance left, then projects forward. Figures are illustrative and assume a floating-rate loan with no prepayment penalty (true for most home loans; fixed-rate and many personal/auto loans charge a fee, so confirm with your lender). If you claim the Section 24(b) interest deduction, heavy prepayment slightly reduces it, though the guaranteed interest saving usually still wins.
